HamThropology

HamThropology is the scientific study of Swine, concerned with swine behavior, swine biology, cultures, and societies, in both the present and past, including past swine species. Helping with this Project gave me the opportunity to learn about the Ossabaw Island Hogs. I was hands on helping with the feeding, breeding, castrating and butchering of these hogs. They were left on an Island by Spanish and Portuguese explorers in the 1500's, a way of securing meat for themselves when they need to restock. As time flowed by these hogs were forgotten about. Untill U.S. pilots flew over them many Centuries later. Ossabaw Island Hogs have adapted to the Brackish waters, heat, humidity, and seasonal scarcity of food on the island.
